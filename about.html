About Me

I’m a graduate student at the University of South Florida specializing in Artificial Intelligence & Business Analytics. I enjoy building practical analytics products—combining solid data engineering, reliable ML/NLP models, and clear stakeholder storytelling—to help teams make faster, better decisions.

My approach is product-oriented: clarify the problem, instrument data pipelines, prototype and evaluate models, and ship a simple interface (dashboard or app) that people actually use. I care about quality, maintainability, and measurable business impact.

Focus Areas

  • End-to-end ML/NLP: data prep, modeling, evaluation, and deployment
  • Cloud data pipelines and automation
  • Executive dashboards (Tableau / Power BI) with clear narratives
  • Responsible, reproducible experimentation and documentation
